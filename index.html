Our story
A Karnataka farmer who got tired of middlemen

My name is S. Umesh Rao. I am a farmer based in Maraluru Village, Gauribidanur, Chikballapur District, Karnataka. For years, I watched middlemen and mandi agents take the largest share of profit from my hard work, leaving farmers like me with barely enough to survive. That had to change.

I grow Moringa oleifera — one of the most nutritious plants in the world — on my 10-acre farm in Karnataka. Fresh drumsticks and moringa pods from my farm reached local mandis every season. But the price I received was always controlled by agents, never by me. So I decided to build a direct supply channel — connecting bulk buyers and drumstick exporters straight to the farm, cutting out every middleman in between.

DrumstickDirect was born from that simple but powerful idea. Today I aggregate fresh drumsticks and moringa pods from my own farm and from trusted farmer networks across Karnataka and Tamil Nadu. I can supply up to 4 tons per day — enough for supermarket chains, hotel kitchens, export companies and online grocery platforms across India.

When you buy from DrumstickDirect, you are not buying from a trader or broker. You are buying directly from a farmer who understands quality, consistency, and the value of a long-term business relationship. My phone is always on. WhatsApp me your drumstick requirement and I will respond personally.
